Section 01 / 05

Hero Section

The highest-stakes real estate on the page. Every word must earn its place. Conversion depends on the first 3 seconds.
The hero section must do four things simultaneously: identify the visitor's pain instantly, promise the transformation, establish credibility, and remove friction from the first click. Our opening is designed to land like a precision instrument — not a sledgehammer.
Label Eyebrow / Pre-Headline
34 chars Authority Exclusivity

AI Meeting Intelligence · Trusted by 4,200+ Teams

Usage: Sits above H1 in smaller caps. Establishes immediate social proof before the visitor reads a single word of pitch copy. The "4,200+ Teams" anchors authority through specificity — precise numbers outperform round numbers.
H1 Primary Headline — Hero
33 chars · 5 words Pain Relief Clarity

Your Meetings, Finally Understood.

Power words: "Finally" — the single most powerful conversion word in SaaS. It acknowledges accumulated frustration without dwelling on it. Implies the prospect has been waiting. The period creates finality and confidence. Avoid: Never lead with the product name or feature list.
Sub Subheadline
117 chars · ~18 words Specificity Authority

ConvoCrystal turns raw transcripts into structured insights, action items, and living documents — in under 60 seconds.

Structure: [Product name] + [mechanism] → [outcome]. The "under 60 seconds" is a quantified promise — specific enough to be credible, fast enough to feel magical. "Living documents" signals ongoing value, not a one-time output. Character target: 100–130 chars. Long enough to inform, short enough to scan.
CTA Primary Call-to-Action Button
28 chars Low Friction Specificity

Start Analyzing Free →

CTA science: Verb-first. "Start" beats "Get" (active vs. passive). "Analyzing" states the exact action, reducing cognitive load. "Free" eliminates the #1 objection before the click. The → arrow signals forward momentum. A/B test against: "Analyze Your First Meeting" and "Try ConvoCrystal Free."
Ghost CTA Secondary Call-to-Action
20 chars Low Commitment

Watch 2-min demo ↗

Role: Captures intent-to-buy visitors who want social proof before committing. "2-min" preempts the objection "I don't have time." Sits visually subordinate to the primary CTA — same line, lower visual weight.

Section 02 / 05

Feature Sections

Three benefit blocks. Each sells the transformation, not the feature. Lead with outcome, reveal the mechanism second.
Features sections fail when they describe what the product does. They convert when they describe what the user becomes. Each block follows the same formula: Outcome headline → Pain acknowledgment → Mechanism → Proof point → Secondary CTA.
H2Section Overline — Above Feature Blocks
52 chars Curiosity

Three things your meeting notes never had before.

Framing: "Never had before" implies breakthrough without making an explicit claim. Counts as both a curiosity hook and an authority signal. Drives scroll into the feature blocks.

Section 04 / 05

FAQ Section

Eight high-intent questions answered with conversion logic. Every answer ends with either a reassurance or a soft CTA nudge.
H2FAQ Section Headline
37 charsObjection Handling

Every question your team will ask. Answered.

Q01 Which meeting platforms does ConvoCrystal support for transcript upload? Integration
ConvoCrystal accepts transcripts from Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, Webex, and any platform that exports a .txt, .vtt, .srt, or .docx file. You can also paste raw text directly or upload a custom JSON transcript format from our API. If you use a platform not listed here, upload the export file and we'll process it — or contact our team for a native integration request. → View all supported formats in our documentation.
Persuasion logic: Lists the top 4 platforms (covers ~90% of the market), then catches the remainder with "any platform." The native integration CTA opens a sales conversation with high-intent users. The doc link reduces churn from confused free-trial users.
Q02 How accurate is the AI analysis? What if it misses something important? Trust / Accuracy
Our AI achieves an average of 91% accuracy on clean transcripts — and every output comes with a confidence score so you always know how much to trust each insight without manual verification. For segments with a score below 70%, ConvoCrystal flags them for human review automatically. You're never flying blind. Accuracy improves the longer ConvoCrystal learns from your team's meeting style — most teams see a marked improvement within 30 sessions. → See how confidence scoring works in a 2-minute walkthrough.
Persuasion logic: The 91% stat is a credible proof point — not 99% (which would be unbelievable) and not vague. The confidence score mention reinforces product differentiation. "Never flying blind" is a powerful anxiety reliever for high-stakes meeting contexts.
Q03 Is my meeting data private? Who can access what I upload? Privacy / Security
Your data is yours. Full stop. ConvoCrystal is SOC 2 Type II certified and fully GDPR-compliant. Transcripts are encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We do not train our models on your data — your meeting content is never used to improve any AI system, including ours. Workspaces are fully isolated: no one outside your team can access your transcripts, analyses, or documents. Enterprise plans include your choice of data residency (US, EU, or APAC). → Read our full security and privacy policy.
Critical for SaaS: This answer must be the most precise on the page. Data anxiety kills trial conversion — especially in enterprise and regulated industries. The phrase "Your data is yours. Full stop." is a confidence-closing statement. "We do not train our models on your data" addresses the #1 privacy fear in AI tools.
Q04 What's included in the free tier? Is there a time limit? Pricing / Free Tier
The free tier is generous by design — and it doesn't expire. You get 5 transcript analyses per month, full access to the live editor and version control, up to 3 document branches, and 30 days of history. No credit card required, no trial countdown, no feature hobbling. We want you to experience the full value of ConvoCrystal before you ever see a pricing page. When you're ready to upgrade, Pro starts at $29/month — and you can cancel anytime. → Start your free account in 60 seconds.
Persuasion logic: "Generous by design" signals intent transparency. Listing features inclusively rather than as a stripped-down comparison to paid tiers creates a positive framing. The pricing mention at the end is a soft anchor — it sets expectation without triggering the "too expensive" reflex.
Q05 How long does it take to analyze a transcript? Is it really instant? Performance
For a typical 60-minute meeting transcript, ConvoCrystal returns a complete structured analysis in under 45 seconds on average — streaming results appear in real time as the analysis runs, so you're never staring at a loading screen. Longer transcripts (2+ hours) process in under 90 seconds. We've optimized the pipeline specifically for the cadence of technical and product workflows — quick enough for post-meeting review before your next call starts. → Watch a live analysis being generated.
Q06 Can I integrate ConvoCrystal with tools my team already uses — Notion, Jira, Slack? Integrations
Yes — and you won't need your IT team involved. ConvoCrystal connects natively to Slack (post summaries directly to channels), Notion (push documents with one click), and exports cleanly to PDF, Markdown, and Jira-compatible JSON. A Jira two-way sync for action items is in active development and launching Q3 2025. All integrations are configured in under 3 minutes from your workspace settings — no code, no webhooks unless you want them. → See the full integration roadmap.
Urgency + Roadmap transparency: Mentioning "Q3 2025" for the Jira sync creates a minor urgency signal and demonstrates that the team is actively building. Roadmap transparency is an exceptionally strong trust signal for technical buyers who fear buying into a dead product.
Q07 What languages does ConvoCrystal support? Localization
ConvoCrystal analyzes transcripts in 28 languages with full AI output in English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Japanese, and Mandarin. Mixed-language meetings (common in global teams) are handled gracefully — the system detects language switches automatically and attributes them to the correct speaker. Full multilingual output in all 28 languages is launching in H2 2025 as part of our Global Teams release. → See supported languages and roadmap.
Q08 How do I get my whole team onboarded without a 2-week rollout project? Adoption / Onboarding
You don't need two weeks. Most teams are fully operational within a single afternoon. Send invites from your dashboard, each member connects in one click, and the first analysis can run the moment their account is verified. ConvoCrystal was designed to be adopted without a rollout plan — it integrates into your existing workflow rather than replacing it. Pro and Team plans include a dedicated onboarding call with a ConvoCrystal specialist — but most teams never need it. → Start a team workspace free — invite up to 3 members at no cost.
Objection removal: Enterprise SaaS adoption anxiety is real. "Designed to be adopted without a rollout plan" is a headline-quality claim that deserves to be tested. The "most teams never need it" line on onboarding support is paradoxically reassuring — it signals the product is intuitive while making the offer sound like a bonus.
